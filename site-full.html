What we do

Four core services, done to street-works standard

Cabinet Replacement

RVI Utility provides a complete cabinet replacement service for street telecoms infrastructure. Our scope covers full removal of existing cabinets, groundworks preparation, new cabinet installation and connection, and site clearance — ensuring every handover meets operator and highway requirements.

Civil Services

Our civil services support a wide range of telecoms and utility construction projects, including site excavation, trench works, chamber installation, duct laying, and ongoing maintenance. We operate nationwide across public highway and private development sites, completing every project to the highest industry standard.

Civils Avoidance

Where possible, RVI employs non-destructive methods to avoid unnecessary excavation. We carry out site surveys, access chamber inspections and pre-construction assessments to identify the safest and most cost-effective route before any groundworks begin.

Footway & Carriageway Reinstatement

Following any excavation or street works, RVI restores footways and carriageways to the required surface standard. All reinstatement is completed in line with NRSWA requirements and local authority specifications, ensuring a clean, compliant finish on every job.
